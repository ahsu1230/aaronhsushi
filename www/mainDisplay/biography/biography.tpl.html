Aaron Hsu

just another guy who dreams about sushi

Born and raised in Maryland, Aaron first started eating sushi at the young age of 10, but it wasn't until he discovered the unique heat from wasabi did he become really into it! His journey in sushi first started when he starting watching youtube videos on basic sushi tutorials. From there, Aaron uses his experiences from various sushi joints to create his own tastes, and styles.

On his free time, Aaron is always thinking about sushi. Whether it is honing his own skills, discovering new recipes, or even just browsing sushi joints online, he loves sushi maybe as much as Gordon Ramsay loves yelling on TV.

Aaron studied Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and now works in Mountain View, California, as a Software Engineer at Kifi.

Other hobbies Aaron enjoys include discovering recipes for his favorite cuisines, boxing, weight-lifting, hip-hop dancing, coding his own projects (like this website), and comic books.
